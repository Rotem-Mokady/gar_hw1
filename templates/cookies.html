Cookies

The best solution for avoiding the loss of a session persistence


The communication between a client and a server is carried out by a communication session.

The session has a limited memory, so after a while the server finally "forgets" about an existence of a client.

Cookie is a small piece of data that contains information for more efficient future identification process of a client by the server.

The cookie has a special template of key's and values, and a special mechanism during an identification process.
